Current mission

Investigating disease‑modifying strategies for Alzheimer’s disease using multimodal molecular imaging. My work combines PET, MRI and MRS with behavioural and biochemical analyses in preclinical AD models.

Skill tree

Multimodal molecular imaging
Design and analysis of longitudinal preclinical imaging studies using PET, MRI and MRS.
Radiochemistry
Radiotracer synthesis, quality control, dose preparation and intravenous administration.
Behavioural assays
Behavioural tests, including Open Field and Barnes Maze.
Biochemical and histological analysis
Ex vivo validation using immunohistochemistry, ELISA, flow cytometry and multiplex cytokine assays.
Experimental surgery
Procedures including stereotaxic surgery, intracranial administration, jugular vein cannulation and microdialysis techniques.
Science communication
Scientific outreach, conference presentations and development of clear data visualisations for effective research communication.
